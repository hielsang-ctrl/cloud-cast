Forecast Overview

Short-term weather planning for four major Kenyan cities.

Cloud Cast focuses on Nairobi, Eldoret, Kisumu, and Nakuru with live current conditions and a short 5-day forecast powered by Open-Meteo.

Nairobi

Urban day-to-day forecast for commuting, events, and outdoor planning.

Eldoret

Highland temperature shifts and likely rain trends across the week.

Kisumu

Lakeside warmth, humidity, and rain probability outlook.

Nakuru

Balanced view of daytime highs, cool nights, and cloud cover changes.

API References

Forecast endpoint: https://api.open-meteo.com/v1/forecast

Documentation: https://open-meteo.com/en/docs

Timezone used: Africa/Nairobi

Data fields used on Cloud Cast: temperature, humidity, weather code, wind speed, daily highs and lows, and precipitation probability.
